About k8s-ai-bench

k8s-ai-bench is a benchmarking tool designed to evaluate Large Language Models (LLMs) on their ability to interact with and manage Kubernetes clusters.

How it works

The benchmark runs a series of 24 predefined tasks against different LLMs. Each task is attempted multiple times to gauge consistency and potential.

Metrics

  • Pass@1: Probability of passing on the first attempt
  • Pass@5: Probability of passing within 5 attempts
  • Pass All 5: Percentage of tasks passed on every single run
